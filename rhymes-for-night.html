What Rhymes With Night?

"Night" is one of the richest words to rhyme in English — it has an enormous number of perfect rhymes, making it a favourite for songwriters, poets, and rappers alike. The "-ight" sound is one of the most common in the English language, which means you'll never be stuck for options.

Perfect Rhymes for Night

The perfect rhymes for "night" are extensive: light, right, bright, fight, flight, might, sight, tight, white, and write are all single-syllable options. Compound words like midnight, moonlight, starlight, tonight, and delight add a poetic, multi-syllable dimension that works beautifully in both songs and poetry.

Night Rhymes in Love Songs

"Night" is one of the most romantic words in music. Pairing it with light, moonlight, tonight, delight, and starlight creates an instantly evocative atmosphere. Near rhymes like shine, divine, and mine extend the palette further, giving you a wide range of options for building a chorus or a bridge.

Night Rhymes in Rap & Hip-Hop

In rap, "night" pairs powerfully with right, fight, ignite, despite, and alright for hard-hitting verses. Slant rhymes like mind, find, blind, and behind add internal complexity and keep the flow unpredictable — a hallmark of sophisticated lyricism.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a perfect rhyme for night?

Perfect rhymes for "night" include light, right, bright, fight, might, sight, tight, white, delight, ignite, tonight, midnight, moonlight, and starlight — all share the exact "-ight" ending sound.

What rhymes with night for a love song?

For a love song, try moonlight, starlight, tonight, delight, or divine — these carry romantic imagery and flow naturally in melodic contexts.

What rhymes with night and day?

"Stay" and "way" rhyme with "day," while "light" and "bright" rhyme with "night" — pairing these creates a classic day/night contrast that works well in poetry and song.

What word rhymes with both night and light?

Any "-ight" word rhymes with both — right, bright, fight, might, sight, tight, white, delight, ignite, and tonight all work for both "night" and "light."
