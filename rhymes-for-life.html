What Rhymes With Life?

"Life" is a powerful word with surprisingly few perfect rhymes — but a rich collection of near rhymes that give songwriters and poets plenty to work with. The key is knowing which category suits your creative context best.

Perfect Rhymes for Life

The perfect rhymes for "life" are limited but meaningful: wife, knife, strife, and rife are the main single-syllable options. Compound words like afterlife, wildlife, nightlife, and midwife extend the list and work particularly well in verses that need a multi-syllable punch.

"Strife" is the most emotionally resonant perfect rhyme for "life" — the pairing of life and strife appears in countless poems and songs, capturing the universal tension between existence and struggle.

Near Rhymes for Life in Songs

The most popular near rhymes for "life" in songs are words ending in "-ive": alive, survive, thrive, drive, arrive, and strive. These carry themes of resilience and forward motion that complement "life" beautifully — and the slight mismatch in sound actually creates a satisfying tension that many listeners find more interesting than a perfect rhyme.

Words like light, night, right, bright, and fight are also widely used near rhymes — they share the long "i" vowel sound and create an energetic, declarative feel.

Life Rhymes in Rap & Hip-Hop

Rap lyrics use "life" extensively in both perfect and slant rhyme schemes. The classic pairing of life / strife / knife / wife appears across genres and decades. Modern rap tends to favour near rhymes like survive, thrive, alive, and drive for their forward momentum, or slant rhymes like mind, find, rise, and eyes for internal complexity.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a perfect rhyme for life?

Perfect rhymes for "life" include wife, knife, strife, rife, afterlife, wildlife, and nightlife — all share the exact "-ife" ending sound.

What rhymes with life for a song?

For songs, near rhymes work beautifully: alive, survive, thrive, drive, strive, and arrive all carry the same long "i" energy as "life" and flow naturally in melodic contexts.

What rhymes with life and death?

"Breath" and "beneath" near-rhyme with "death," while "strife," "knife," and "wife" rhyme with "life." Pairing these in a verse creates a powerful life-and-death thematic contrast.

What word rhymes with life in rap?

In rap, the most popular rhymes for "life" are strife, knife, wife, alive, survive, thrive, and drive — these appear across decades of hip-hop in both classic and contemporary verses.
