What Rhymes With Heart?

"Heart" is one of the most emotionally powerful words in the English language — and fortunately, it has a rich collection of perfect rhymes, near rhymes, and slant rhymes to work with. Whether you're writing a love song, a breakup anthem, or a poem about resilience, you'll find the right rhyme here.

Perfect Rhymes for Heart

The perfect rhymes for "heart" are plentiful and expressive: art, start, part, apart, smart, chart, and depart are all single or two-syllable options. Compound words like sweetheart, upstart, and counterpart add sophistication and work particularly well in verses that need a multi-syllable landing.

The pairing of heart / apart is one of the most classic in all of songwriting — it captures the pain of separation in just two words, which is why it appears in countless love songs across every genre.

Near Rhymes for Heart in Songs

Near rhymes open up the emotional landscape considerably. Words like dark, spark, star, scar, and far share the open "a" vowel sound with "heart" and carry vivid imagery. "Spark" in particular pairs beautifully with "heart" thematically — the spark that starts in the heart is a classic romantic metaphor.

Surprisingly, love, above, and dove are also used as near rhymes for "heart" in songs — the emotional connection between the words compensates for the imperfect sound match.

Heart Rhymes in Rap & Hip-Hop

In rap, "heart" pairs powerfully with start, apart, smart, dart, and impart for direct, punchy verses. Slant rhymes like hurt, dirt, worth, earth, and birth add a raw, introspective quality — they feel emotionally honest rather than technically precise, which suits confessional rap particularly well.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a perfect rhyme for heart?

Perfect rhymes for "heart" include art, start, part, apart, smart, chart, dart, depart, sweetheart, and counterpart — all share the exact "-art" ending sound.

What rhymes with heart for a love song?

For a love song, try apart, start, sweetheart, spark, star, or dark — these carry romantic imagery and emotional weight that complement "heart" beautifully.

What rhymes with heart and soul?

"Soul" rhymes with whole, role, goal, and control. For a verse using both "heart" and "soul," try pairing heart/apart and soul/whole in alternating lines — a classic structure in gospel, R&B, and pop ballads.

What word rhymes with broken heart?

For "broken heart," try "torn apart," "falling apart," or "from the start" — these multi-word phrases match the three-syllable rhythm of "broken heart" and carry complementary emotional weight.
