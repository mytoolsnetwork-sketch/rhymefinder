What Rhymes With Time?

"Time" is one of the most rhyme-friendly words in English — it has dozens of perfect rhymes and hundreds of near rhymes. This makes it a favourite in songwriting, poetry, and rap. Whether you need a single-syllable punch or a multi-syllable flow, "time" gives you plenty of options.

Perfect Rhymes for Time

The perfect rhymes for "time" include everyday words like rhyme, climb, crime, prime, and sublime as well as compound words like lifetime, daytime, nighttime, overtime, and anytime. These compound rhymes are particularly useful in rap and hip-hop where multi-syllable rhymes create a more sophisticated flow.

Near Rhymes for Time in Songs

Near rhymes open up a huge range of possibilities. Words ending in "-ine" like fine, mine, shine, divine, and wine are particularly popular in love songs and ballads — they share the long "i" sound with "time" and flow naturally in melodic contexts.

Words like night, light, right, and side are also widely used near rhymes — they create a bright, forward-moving sound that works well in upbeat songs and anthems.

Time Rhymes in Rap & Hip-Hop

Rap lyrics use "time" extensively — both as a perfect rhyme and as part of multi-syllable schemes. Pairing "time" with "crime," "paradigm," and "sublime" is a classic rap move. Slant rhymes like mind, find, kind, and behind add internal complexity and keep verses from feeling too predictable.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a perfect rhyme for time?

Perfect rhymes for "time" include rhyme, climb, crime, dime, lime, prime, sublime, lifetime, daytime, and overtime — all share the exact "-ime" ending sound.

What rhymes with time for a love song?

For a love song, try "sublime," "divine," "mine," "shine," or "fine" — these near rhymes carry romantic connotations and flow naturally in melodic contexts.

What rhymes with time in rap?

Rap-friendly rhymes for time include crime, paradigm, sublime, and overtime for perfect rhymes, plus mind, find, behind, and design for slant rhymes that add internal complexity.

What word rhymes with time and life?

"Strife" rhymes with "life" and near-rhymes with "time" — useful for a two-line verse. "Prime" rhymes with "time" and pairs well thematically with "life" in the same verse.
